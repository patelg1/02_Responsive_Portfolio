About Me

I have dabbled in development in my free time, learning languages such as Java and Python. Some technologies and skills I have learned are HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Bulma, jQuery, JSON, API's, AJAX, Node.js, Command Line, and Git.
